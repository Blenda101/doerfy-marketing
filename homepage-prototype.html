How it Works

Three things.
One practice.

Doerfy is not a task manager with extra steps. It is a system that begins with your Vision and ends with your daily work—connected, committed, and in rhythm.

01
Design Phase — Theme Builder

Design your Theme.

Before you manage tasks, you author your life. A Theme is the master story that unifies your career, health, relationships, and craft—not as separate tabs, but as one life lived on purpose. The Four ACTs (Vision, Mission, Values, Strategy) give your Theme structure. Doey helps you find yours.

Meet Doey

Your coach.
Not a chatbot.

Doey knows your Theme, your Outcomes, your patterns, and your commitments. Doey asks more than it tells. It holds the structure so you can focus on the work.

Persistent across all spaces. Doey remembers your Vision when you're buried in a task, and remembers the task when you're dreaming about the future.

Framework-first. Doey operates within the Doerfy Method. It won't tell you to hustle harder. It will ask where that aligns with your Theme.

Warm but direct. Short sentences. No flattery. Doey talks like a coach who has earned your trust—not a product that wants you to feel good about not doing the work.

The Doer Manifesto

Ten principles.
The bedrock.

Doerfy is built on a creed. Not a list of values—a set of beliefs about what a life is for. These are not aspirational. They are the truth we build from.

I.

Design the life you truly desire.

A life that is not designed is a life that is defaulted into. Before anything else—before the goals, before the work—the Doer answers the only question that matters.

II.

Visualize what you have designed.

A vision unseen will not be lived. The Doer renders the designed life into something the eye can hold. What is rendered is remembered. What is remembered is pursued.

III.

Live by Theme.

Career, family, health, craft, money, spirit—these are not separate lives competing for attention. They are domains within the one life the Theme designs.

X.

Life is a reflection of what you do.

Not of what you intended. Not of what you imagined. The Doer's life is the visible record of the actions taken, the work shipped, the days lived.
